Mission Statement

Our mission is to Reach the Lost -- those who are devoid of Christ; Reach the Least -- those who are devoid of money and possessions; and Reach the Last -- those who are devoid of social status, with The Message of Faith and the loving touch to help men and women overcome the challenges of life. This church is to provide enrichment through special emphasis ministry to fulfill our goals. The Fruit of the Ministry should be that lives of people will be victoriously transformed by The Word and they take the same message into their worlds.
